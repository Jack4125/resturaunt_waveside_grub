How We Started

Waveside Grill was started by two friends who grew up in Santa Barbara and loved barbecue. The business initially consisted of catering and dishing up deliciousness for local businesses and festivals; in 2020, we set up everything we needed to serve whole Santa Barbara community.

Today we welcome customers from across Santa Barbara county. With local owners, we know what people in the area want when it comes to their steak and barbecue, and we're ready to serve it up!
